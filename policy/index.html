Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in Growdie Private Server.

Introduction

Welcome to Growdie Private Server ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

By accessing or using Growdie Private Server, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our service to you:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your username, email address, and password (hashed and encrypted).
  • Game Data: We store your in-game progress, items, world data, and player statistics.
  • Connection Information: IP addresses, device information, and connection timestamps for security purposes.
  • Payment Information: For premium services, we process payments through secure third-party providers and do not store full payment details.

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide, maintain, and improve our services:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide gameplay services and maintain your game progress
  • To detect and prevent cheating, hacking, and other violations
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To send important notices about service changes
  • For analytics to improve our services

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • All sensitive data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with unique salts
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Limited access to personal data for staff members

While we strive to protect your information, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we implement industry-standard protections.

Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies for:

  • Payment Processing: PayPal, Stripe, or other payment gateways (we don't store your full payment details)
  • Analytics: Google Analytics to understand service usage
  • Hosting: Cloud service providers to run our servers

These third parties have access only to the information needed to perform their functions and are obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes.

Server Rules & Community Guidelines

To maintain a fair and enjoyable environment for all players, we enforce these rules:

Prohibited Activities

  • No cheating or hacking: Using third-party software to gain unfair advantages
  • No exploiting bugs: Report bugs instead of abusing them
  • No harassment: Racism, sexism, hate speech, or personal attacks
  • No scamming: Deceptive practices to steal items or accounts
  • No spamming: Excessive messages or advertisements
  • No inappropriate content: Offensive names, worlds, or imagery
  • No account sharing: Your account is your responsibility

Violations may result in temporary suspensions, permanent bans, or account termination at our discretion. Severe violations may be reported to authorities.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "effective date" at the bottom.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

  • Email: growdieofficial@gmail.com
  • Discord: Growdie Support Server
  • In-game: Contact any staff member with @Admin tag

We're committed to resolving any concerns about your privacy and our collection of data. Please reach out with any questions.
